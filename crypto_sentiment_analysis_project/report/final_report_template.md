## Objective
Analyze the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment and trader performance.

## Key Questions
1. Does trader profitability change during Fear vs Greed?
2. Do traders use higher leverage during Greed?

- Fear periods reduce overtrading
- Profitable traders manage leverage better
